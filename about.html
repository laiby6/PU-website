About Us

It is for the first time in 124 year history of the Punjab University that it has established a teaching campus outside Lahore. Gujranwala Campus has been established with the keen interest & support of the Honourable & Worthy Vice-Chancellor. The opening of the sub-campus is in accordance with the educational policy of the present regime of President Islamic Republic of Pakistan, who is taking a keen interest in promoting education.
